Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the WaterMii mobile application ("Application") developed by Dorking Apps ("Service Provider"). The Application is provided as an ad-supported service and is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application may collect limited technical information automatically such as:

  • Device IP address
  • Device operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Time spent using the Application

This information is used only for analytics and improving the Application.

Health Data

The Application allows users to manually log their water intake. This information is considered Health Data.

The Application stores the following health-related data:

  • Daily water intake records entered by the user
  • Hydration tracking history

All water intake data is stored locally on the user's device. The Service Provider does not collect, transmit, or store this health data on external servers.

This data is used only to display hydration progress and reminders within the Application.

Third-Party Services

The Application may use third-party services for advertising.

  • Google AdMob

These services may collect anonymized information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

Water intake data and user preferences are stored locally on the user's device for as long as the Application remains installed.

Data Deletion

Users can delete their data by:

  • Clearing the app's data from device settings
  • Uninstalling the Application

Once the Application is uninstalled or app data is cleared, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Children

The Application is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because hydration data is stored locally on the device, users maintain full control over their information.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
